Top 10 Parks in Gujarat

1. Kankaria Lake & Park, Ahmedabad

Location: Maninagar, Ahmedabad

Details: Scenic lakefront park with zoo, train ride, amusement zone, and boat rides.

Rating: ★★★★★

Contact: +91-79-2546-4481

More Info: Wikipedia

2. Sayaji Garden (Kamati Baug), Vadodara

Location: Near Sayaji Railway Station, Vadodara

Details: Huge garden with museum, zoo, toy train, and planetarium.

Rating: ★★★★☆

More Info: Wikipedia

3. Indroda Nature Park, Gandhinagar

Location: Sector 7, Gandhinagar

Details: India's only dinosaur-themed park with botanical gardens and zoo.

Rating: ★★★★☆

Contact: +91-79-232-53828

More Info: Wikipedia

4. Vastrapur Lake Park, Ahmedabad

Location: Vastrapur, Ahmedabad

Details: Lakeside park with walking trails, amphitheater, and light shows.

Rating: ★★★★☆

More Info: Wikipedia

5. Children's Park, Gandhinagar

Location: Near Sector 28, Gandhinagar

Details: Great for families with boating, toy trains, and amusement rides.

Rating: ★★★★☆

6. Riverfront Park, Ahmedabad

Location: Along Sabarmati River, Ahmedabad

Details: Modern urban park with jogging tracks, gardens, flower shows, and events.

Rating: ★★★★★

More Info: Wikipedia

7. Jubilee Garden, Rajkot

Location: Near Watson Museum, Rajkot

Details: Historic garden with monuments, toy train, and cultural exhibitions.

Rating: ★★★☆☆

8. Aji Dam Garden, Rajkot

Location: Near Aji Dam, Rajkot

Details: Peaceful green space with fountains and picnic areas.

Rating: ★★★★☆

9. Sarthana Nature Park, Surat

Location: Sarthana Jakatnaka, Surat

Details: Park with zoo, lush green environment, and walking tracks.

Rating: ★★★★☆

10. Victoria Park, Bhavnagar

Location: Bhavnagar

Details: Forested area ideal for bird watching and nature walks.

Rating: ★★★★☆

More Info: Wikipedia
