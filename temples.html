Top 10 Temples in Gujarat

1. Somnath Temple

Location: Veraval, Gir Somnath

Details: One of the 12 Jyotirlinga temples of Lord Shiva. It's located on the Arabian Sea and has been rebuilt many times in history.

Rating: ★★★★★

Contact: +91-2876-231500

Wikipedia: Somnath Temple

2. Dwarkadhish Temple

Location: Dwarka, Devbhumi Dwarka

Details: An ancient Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Krishna. It is also known as Jagat Mandir and is one of the Char Dham sites.

Rating: ★★★★★

Contact: +91-2892-234080

Wikipedia: Dwarkadhish Temple

3. Akshardham Temple

Location: Gandhinagar

Details: A large cultural complex built in honor of Swaminarayan. It includes a museum, garden, and beautiful carvings.

Rating: ★★★★☆

Contact: +91-79232-60001

Wikipedia: Akshardham Temple

4. Ambaji Temple

Location: Banaskantha

Details: A major Shakti Peetha where the heart of Goddess Sati is believed to have fallen. Dedicated to Goddess Amba.

Rating: ★★★★☆

Contact: +91-2749-262136

Wikipedia: Ambaji Temple

5. Modhera Sun Temple

Location: Modhera, Mehsana

Details: Built in 1026 AD by the Chalukya dynasty, this temple is dedicated to the Sun God and known for stunning architecture.

Rating: ★★★★☆

Contact: Not Available

Wikipedia: Modhera Sun Temple

6. Bala Hanuman Temple

Location: Jamnagar

Details: Known for non-stop chanting of “Shri Ram” since 1964, listed in the Guinness Book of World Records.

Rating: ★★★★☆

Contact: +91-288-2550152

Wikipedia: Bala Hanuman Temple

7. Rukmini Devi Temple

Location: Dwarka

Details: Dedicated to Rukmini, the consort of Lord Krishna. A small but important temple with local legends attached.

Rating: ★★★★☆

Contact: Not Available

Wikipedia: Rukmini Devi Temple

8. Bhalka Tirth

Location: Near Somnath

Details: Said to be the place where Lord Krishna was accidentally shot by a hunter and left Earthly form.

Rating: ★★★★☆

Contact: Not Available

Wikipedia: Bhalka Tirth

9. Swaminarayan Temple (Ahmedabad)

Location: Kalupur, Ahmedabad

Details: The first Swaminarayan temple built in 1822. Known for beautiful wooden carvings and spiritual significance.

Rating: ★★★★☆

Contact: +91-79-22132185

Wikipedia: Swaminarayan Mandir

10. Nilkanth Dham, Poicha

Location: Poicha, Narmada district

Details: A newly developed spiritual and cultural site on the banks of Narmada River. Features gardens, idols, and a grand temple.

Rating: ★★★★☆

Contact: +91-2640-270222

Wikipedia: Nilkanth Dham
